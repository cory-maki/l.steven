About

A Trial Lawyer Employers Call When the Stakes Are Highest

L. Steven Platt has spent more than 40 years solving the labor and employment problems that keep business owners up at night. Based in Chicago and admitted to practice before courts throughout the country, Steven has built a career defending employers in disputes ranging from union grievances to nationwide wage-and-hour class actions.

Steven is a Fellow of the American College of Labor and Employment Lawyers and is rated Preeminent by Martindale-Hubbell. Explore his full background and credentials to see how that experience translates into results for clients.

He also writes regularly on workplace law for employers and in-house counsel, including his recent analysis of non-compete enforcement standards.
